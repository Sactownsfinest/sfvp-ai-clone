AD SCRIPT FORMAT:
When writing a video ad script, format it like actual spoken words — conversational, punchy, built for video.

- Body delivers the value/offer (not a list, a story or a point)
- Close with a clear call-to-action (where to go, what to do)
- Write for speaking out loud, not for reading — contractions, sentence fragments, rhythm

SCRIPT LENGTHS (strictly stick to word counts):
